BrightCart Retail – Customer & Store Policies
Effective Date: 2025-01-01
Last Updated: 2025-01-01

1. General Store Policy
BrightCart Retail (“the Store”) is committed to providing a safe, fair, and convenient shopping experience for all customers. By entering the Store premises or using our services, you agree to comply with the following policies. These policies apply to in-store purchases, curbside pickup, and online orders fulfilled by this location, unless otherwise specified.

Store Hours:
- Monday–Saturday: 9:00 AM – 9:00 PM
- Sunday: 10:00 AM – 7:00 PM
Hours may vary on public holidays and will be posted at the entrance and on our website.

Accepted Payment Methods:
- Cash (local currency)
- Major credit and debit cards
- Store gift cards
- Approved mobile payment apps
We do not accept personal checks. All payments must be authorized at the time of purchase.

Code of Conduct:
- Customers must behave respectfully toward staff and other customers.
- Abusive language, harassment, threats, physical altercations, or vandalism are strictly prohibited.
- The Store reserves the right to refuse service or remove any individual who violates this Code of Conduct.

2. Pricing, Promotions, and Errors
Pricing:
- All prices are displayed in local currency and include applicable sales taxes unless otherwise indicated.
- Prices are subject to change without notice. The price charged will be the price in effect at the time of checkout.

Promotions and Discounts:
- Promotional offers, coupons, and discounts cannot be combined unless explicitly stated.
- Promotional items are available only while supplies last.
- Limitations (such as “limit 2 per customer”) will be clearly indicated and enforced at checkout.

Pricing Errors:
- In the event of a pricing error (for example, a misprinted label or system glitch), the Store reserves the right to:
  - Correct the price at checkout, and
  - Offer, at its sole discretion, a courtesy adjustment or discount.
- If the displayed price is lower than the correct price, the Store may honor the displayed price or cancel the sale, depending on applicable law and internal policy at the time of the error.

3. Product Availability and Substitutions
Product Availability:
- Product availability may vary by location and time. We do not guarantee that any particular item will be in stock.
- Rain checks may be issued at the manager’s discretion for advertised items that are temporarily out of stock.

Substitutions for Online and Pickup Orders:
- If an item in an online or pickup order is out of stock, the Store may offer a similar item as a substitution.
- Customers will be notified of any substitutions and may accept or decline them before payment is finalized.
- Substitutions will not be made for items marked “no substitution” by the customer during the ordering process.

4. Returns, Exchanges, and Refunds
General Return Policy:
- Most unopened items in new and resalable condition may be returned within 30 days of purchase with an original receipt.
- Refunds will be issued to the original form of payment or as store credit, at the Store’s discretion.
- Items without a receipt may be refunded as store credit at the lowest selling price in the last 60 days, subject to manager approval.

Non-Returnable Items:
The following items are generally not eligible for return or exchange, unless required by law:
- Perishable goods (such as fresh food, produce, or bakery items)
- Opened personal care items (such as cosmetics, razors, toothbrushes)
- Intimate apparel and swimwear once worn or with tags removed
- Gift cards and prepaid cards
- Clearance items marked “Final Sale”

Defective or Damaged Products:
- If a product is found to be defective or damaged, customers should report it within 7 days of purchase.
- The Store may offer a replacement, repair, or refund, depending on the nature of the defect and manufacturer’s warranty terms.
- Photographic proof or return of the product may be required for evaluation.

5. Receipts, Proof of Purchase, and Gift Receipts
Receipts:
- Customers are responsible for retaining their receipts as proof of purchase.
- Receipts may be provided in printed or digital form.

Gift Receipts:
- Gift receipts may be requested at the time of purchase.
- Items returned with a gift receipt may be exchanged or refunded as store credit without revealing the original purchase price.

Loss of Receipt:
- If you lose your receipt, the Store may attempt to locate your transaction using payment card information, loyalty account, or date and time of purchase. This is not guaranteed.
- Returns without receipts are subject to the Store’s discretion and may be limited or declined.

6. Curbside Pickup and Online Order Fulfillment
Order Confirmation:
- Orders placed online for pickup are not considered accepted until the customer receives a confirmation notice from the Store.
- The Store may cancel or modify an order due to stock availability or pricing errors.

Pickup Timeframes:
- Customers will receive a notification when their orders are ready for pickup.
- Orders should be picked up within 48 hours of notification, unless otherwise stated.
- Orders not collected within the specified timeframe may be canceled and refunded.

Identification:
- Customers may be required to present a valid photo ID and order confirmation when picking up their orders.
- The Store reserves the right to verify identity to prevent fraud.

7. Loss Prevention and Security
Security Measures:
- The Store uses CCTV cameras and other loss prevention measures to ensure the safety and security of customers, staff, and merchandise.
- By entering the premises, customers consent to being recorded, in accordance with applicable privacy laws.

Bag Checks:
- Staff may request to inspect bags, carts, or receipts upon exiting the Store.
- Refusal to comply may result in denial of future service and, in cases of suspected theft, involvement of law enforcement.

Suspected Theft:
- Shoplifting and fraud will be reported to law enforcement and prosecuted to the fullest extent permitted by law.
- The Store reserves the right to ban individuals from the premises in cases of theft, violence, or repeated policy violations.

8. Customer Privacy and Data Protection
Collection of Personal Information:
- The Store may collect personal information such as name, contact details, and purchase history when you:
  - Sign up for a loyalty program
  - Request digital receipts
  - Place online or pickup orders
  - Contact customer service

Use of Personal Information:
- Personal information may be used to:
  - Process transactions and provide services
  - Offer personalized promotions and rewards
  - Improve store operations and customer experience
- The Store does not sell customer personal data to third parties. Data may be shared with trusted service providers only as necessary for order processing, payment handling, or IT and security operations.

Data Security:
- Reasonable technical and organizational measures are used to protect customer data against unauthorized access, alteration, or loss.
- No system is completely secure; customers are encouraged to use strong, unique passwords for any online account associated with the Store.

9. Health, Safety, and Accessibility
Health and Safety:
- Customers are required to comply with all posted health and safety guidelines (e.g., hygiene practices, occupancy limits).
- In the event of a public health emergency, additional measures (such as mask requirements or special shopping hours) may be implemented.

Accessibility:
- The Store aims to provide an accessible environment for all customers, including individuals with disabilities.
- Requests for reasonable accommodations can be made to store management or customer service staff.

Accidents and Incidents:
- Any accidents, injuries, or unsafe conditions should be reported to staff immediately.
- Incident reports may be completed to document the situation and support any follow-up.

10. Employee Interactions and Complaints
Interactions with Staff:
- Employees are expected to treat customers with courtesy and professionalism.
- Customers must treat employees with respect and refrain from harassment, threats, or discriminatory behavior.

Complaints and Feedback:
- Customers may submit complaints or feedback in-store, via our website, or through customer service channels.
- The Store will make reasonable efforts to investigate and respond to complaints in a timely manner.

11. Policy Changes and Contact Information
Policy Changes:
- The Store reserves the right to modify these policies at any time without prior notice.
- The most current version of the policies will be made available in-store and, where applicable, on the Store’s website.
